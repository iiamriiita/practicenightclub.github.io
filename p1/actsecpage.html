BABOOM REST IN PEACE


 ▶ Play  


Apr 1st       23PM-3AM

Line up
_____________________________
DJ Qsun / Marry Wong / Peter Poka / DJ Yoyo


與我們團聚在一起，向掙脫囚禁的勇敢狒狒致敬。18天來，儘管背負著世人對他的誤解，但他在自己的冒險中没有造成任何傷害。他只是想看看這個世界，他渴望自由。本周六，讓我們慶祝他的精神，並提高對動物權益保護的意識。

Gather with us to pay tribute to the brave baboon who broke free from captivity. For 18 days, despite the world's misconceptions, he harmed no one on his adventure. He simply wanted to see the world. He wanted to be free. This Saturday, let's celebrate his spirit and raise awareness for the importance of animal freedom and welfare.
