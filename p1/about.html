Studio9


The best techno space in Taipie

𝐒𝐭𝐮𝐝𝐢𝐨 𝟗 是一個全新的獨立電子音樂場域，位於台北市西門町的中心，為音樂愛好者提供前衛的 𝐇𝐨𝐮𝐬𝐞 和 𝐓𝐞𝐜𝐡𝐧𝐨 音樂，擁有超過百坪的室內外寬敞空間，分隔成三個不同特色的區塊。
Studio 9 is a new venue atop a building in the heart of Ximen, offering high quality underground music and a space for people to socialize, relax, and see and be seen.With a spacious space, it is separated into three different sections.

平日的時候，Studio 9 是一個坐擁日落戶外露台的音樂休閒餐酒吧。而在周末，Studio 9 將轉變為一個音樂展演場所，為音樂愛好者提供前衛的 House 和 Techno 音樂。 整體空間的設計風格與典型的地下俱樂部相去甚遠。藉由現代藝術和多彩的裝飾，Studio 9 正在為台北音樂文化創造一個全新的概念和氛圍。 品牌創立團隊有個清晰的願景，就是讓這裡成為西門町新穎的國際化的熱點。現場使用高品質的 Martin Audio 音響陣列系統，結合有機裝飾和視覺藝術帶來獨特的音樂娛樂體驗。

The venue will operate as a lounge bar and sunset terrace during weekdays, and on weekends Studio 9 will transform to a event venue for house and techno music lovers. The interior design drifts away from your typical underground club style you are are most accustomed too. Playing with a modern art and colorful decoration, Studio 9 is creating a fresh and new atmosphere for clubbing in Taipei. The founders behind the project have a clear vision to make this place a new hot spot in Ximen. Providing a high quality Martin Audio sound system combined with its organic decoration and visual art installation.
